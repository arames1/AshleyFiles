Ticket Information TEDxSVSU 2017

Tickets are very limited and cost $25 USD or $30 CAD

TEDxSVSU will be held at Saginaw Valley State University in Curtis Hall and the conference will be in Banquet Rooms A & B located on the second floor.

Follow the signs from the main entrance of Curtis Hall.

Parking is available in front of Curtis Hall in lots J1 and J2.

The doors will be open at 5:00pm and attendees are encouraged to explore the exhibits outside of the Banquet Rooms. Experience virtual reality, learn about wireless security, and become familiar with credit card sized computing! Food and snacks will be provided before and during the conference. Please be courteous to the speakers and stay seated until their Talk has concluded before standing.

You may pay for your tickets in either cash or credit online or in our offices at Curtis Hall prior to the show.
